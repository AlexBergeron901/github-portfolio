My Projects

Manly Idiot Launches Far

In this VR game combining platforming and psychological horror, you control a person sitting on an office chair equipped with a suction cup, whose goal is to reach the top. However, a mysterious television equip with propeller, acting as your narrator, has only one purpose: to make you give up the climb by distracting you, getting in your way, or demoralizing you whenever you fall. In Manly Idiot Launches Far, we were required to use several technologies, including creating a mobile application linked to the game, integrating haptic feedback vests, using connected objects, and implementing the MQTT protocol. My role in designing this game was to create the mobile app that serves as the game's leaderboard, implement the haptic vests to simulate vertigo, and set up MQTT to allow users to connect to the game with their phones and link connected objects to the experience. In addition, I worked with the team on several other aspects, such as implementing player movement, configuring the VR headset and controllers, and writing and recording the narrative content. This project allowed me to learn the basics of VR game programming and the integration of external technologies within a game.
